Monchi Advertisement campaign

Background Info

Monchi’s branding appeals to their younger audiences, with the use of soft pastel colours and rounded fonts. On their Instagram posts, they often highlight on the photography of Monchi, using texts and secondary props to draw attention to their product.

Role

  • To research and sell the product

  • To deliver 3 Advertisement Posters and Instagram Carousel Post

  • To write up copywriting

Unique Selling Point

The three unique selling points of Monchi are Companionship, being your child’s new best friend and creating a fun snacking experience Mess-free, reduces the amount of waste and cleaning up needed Convenient, as it is a lunchbox, utensil and entertainer all in one!

Objective

To sell Monchi as a "All in one" Box We wanted to show the different aspects Monchi is capable of. Even if it seems small, having all of your problems snacking outside fixed by a small box can make a big difference in convenience.

Our Research

Research Goals

  • Understand about what Monchi is

  • Identify the Target Tribe

  • Learn about the benefit of using Monchi

Target Tribe

The target tribes for Monchi. Concerned parents who want to let their children snack without the hassle and mess that often comes with it and are tired of cleaning up constantly. Clumsy Children who do not care about cleanliness when eating and is merely focused on enjoying their food Playful kids who love playing and would find everything and anything to play with. For our concept, we decided to go for a superhero theme, whereby Monchi is portrayed as a superhero where its features are Monchi’s superpowers, saving people from the snacktime mess

Key Message, Sketch, Comp up

Key Messages

  • Monchi Saves the Day!

  • Catch the Crumb

  • Swerve the Spill

  • Grip the Treat

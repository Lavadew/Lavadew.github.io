Blessed Yacht

Introduction

Blessed Yacht is a 43ft Meridian 391 Flybridge Cabin Cruiser with a 360-degree panoramic view at the fly bridge on the upper deck. This luxurious boat comes with a sea lift and can cater up to 15 guests. This beautiful yacht houses 2 spacious cabins and a luxuriously large, air-conditioned saloon that is equipped with a 32” plasma television and karaoke sound system. The galley at the saloon includes a refrigerator and microwave oven. In other words, it is almost like a condo on the waves. Berthed at Marina @ Keppel Bay, this elegant yacht is suitable for friends, family and corporate clients for any celebratory events or corporate team building.


Target Audience

  • Corporate clients
  • Private Events (wedding proposal, birthdays etc)
  • Families
  • People of all age

Objective

To promote their boat rentals and showcase clients’ testimonials.


My Involvement

I have to conceptualize and design all the subpages with the user experience in mind. The client specifically say they want a luxury look and feel to the website so I kept that in mind when I was designing the website.

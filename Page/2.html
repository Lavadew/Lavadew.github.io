Backgroud Info

Adidas is a German manufacturer of sportswear. It is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe and the second largest in the world. Adidas is well-known for their sports shoes as they implement new technology. As a result, Adidas dominate the soccer scene with their shoes.

Objective

To create 3 Adidas Products for 3 Different Sports and deliver them as a Campaign Poster Series.

Our Research

Research Goals

  • Understand about Adidas Marketing Strategy

  • Identify the Target Tribe

  • Learn about Competitors

Target Tribe

Our target tribe are called “Adidudes” who are in their 20s and passionate about sports. We want to bring out the best of our athletes using our product therefore we want to showcase the technology of our shoes. As Adidas slogan is “Impossible is Nothing” & “Adidas is All in” , we want our tribe to believe in the technology of our shoes, in terms of performance, comfortability and durability to bring out their true potential in their sports.

Key Message, Sketch, Comp up

Key Messages

  • Rise to the Top

  • Go Beyond Your Limits

  • Achieving Greatness

Final Outcome

The Rationale behind this series is to invoke a sense of power to dominate their respective sports wearing Adidas Shoes. We want the user to know the Adidas Shoes will lead them to their dream and also convey a message that they only need to focus in their sports without worrying about their equipment.

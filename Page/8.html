Feng Xiang Advertisement

Introduction

Feng Xiang is a new F&B brand in Singapore, gradually expanding its reach in the competitive market. Its key offerings of traditional, authentic Klang-style dishes may be unfamiliar to local diners.


Objective

Create a set of brand identity that resonates with diners and partners, blending both traditional and contemporary. Generate better brand awareness and brand recall among diners. Push for consistency between brand touchpoints, which is important as the business continues to expand.


My Involvement

To create a Video showcasing Feng Xiang's Bak Kut Teh Dishes and promote the stores located throughout Singapore. This Video, will be showcase in QB House. To create a Menu showcasing their Frog Series Dishes as well as Article Banners for their Website.

Brand Campaign

QB House Video

How it come about

This was a quick project due to client needing this project fast and delivered, I have to think of ways to make delivering the video as soon as possible.

I decided that the best way would to make it into an infographic video with a Hook Video at the start.

The Challenges

I have to use videos from their social media, which is compressed and very blurry. Due to that I have to think of ways to sharpen the video, and I try to do it by sharpening and turning up the contrast as well as the vibrancy of the video. I also reduce noises to keep the quality. The animation part of things was simple, only thing that hinders me is the amount of layers I have to control in After Effects

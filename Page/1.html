WhatIf Food Brand/Web Design

Background Info

WhatIF Food’s goal is to create tasty and nutritious foods that nourish us and nurture the planet using Future Crop. WhatIF Food’s brand image is very friendly as the brand uses natural and plant based ingredients,eco friendly materials was used for their packagings as well.

Role

  • To revamp WhatIf Foods Brand and create a brand identity

  • To create a consistent branding style guide throughout all the design

  • To revamp and improve WhatIf Food Website

Background Info

Whatif Foods aim to not focus on sustainability but regenerating.The ReGeneration is a way of life that reflects a set of attitudes, beliefs, and worldviews that drive an individual's actions toward replenishment rather than depletion, restoration rather than degradation, and reconnection rather than dissociation.

Objective

To re-brand, conceptualize, create packaging and provide them with a new identity

Our Research

Research Goals

  • Understand the Brand Personality

  • Identify the Target Tribe

  • Learn about Competitors

Insights

WhatIF products are designed to have the color of the rainbow from red hot tomato soup, to green moringa noodles, and to purple ube shake.Also to make the products more appealing Consumers are encouraged to be creative with their products. For example, the WhatIF Apple Cinnamon Shake Powder can be mixed with other ingredients to make Apple Cinnamon Sultana Muffins.

We want to target the "Rebels", who are 18 to 40 year olds who identify with the rebeloutionary message.

Competitors:


YOUMEE Through our research we have found competitor brand which is Youmee.This brand have similar aims which is procuse tasty and healthy comfort foods .

One Culture Food Through our research we have found competitor brand which is One Culture Food.This brand have similar aims which is procuse tasty and healthy comfort foods .

Key Message & Art Direction

Key Messages

We want to project the brand as a revolutionary/rebellious brand that goes against the grain.


  • Addictive

    Colours can be very appetizing and eye catching Use attractive imagery such as close up Slurping

  • Comedic

    Playful graphics to bring out the energetic and fun vibes Food puns Bubbly Fonts

  • Effortless

    Simple, Easy to understand Packaging Less Wordy Easy to prepare Convenient to use /dispose

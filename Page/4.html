Bak Kut Teh Infographic & Animation

Background Info

This was a class assignment, whereby we are required to create an infographic and a 2d animation infographic video on Local Cuisine / Musical Instrumental / Doctor Equipment. I decided to do one of my favourite local cuisine "Bak Kut Teh" and tries to learn more about the dishes.

Role

  • To research on Bak Kut Teh

  • To create an infographic relating to the research I have gathered

  • To create an 2d Animation infographic

Our Research

Research Goals

  • find out how Bak Kut Teh is made

  • Find other Variations of Bak Kut Teh

  • Purposes of the Bak Kut

Insights

Bak Kut Teh is thought to have originated in the China province of Fujian. Hokkien immigrants who relocated to this country in the nineteenth century are credited with the introduction of the dish to Singapore and Malaysia. The dish is known to have been popular among Chinese coolies as a nutritious breakfast before beginning their day's backbreaking labor.

Variations:

With growing affluence, two streams of bak kut teh emerged in Singapore. The Hokkien folks added Chinese herbs like dang gui and liquorice, and spices such as cinnamon and star anise to the garlic and soy sauce.


Purposes:

For the poor coolies, there was little to eat. No pork (meat) soup but soup made of garlic cloves, soy sauce and pork bones with scraps of meat on special days, maybe.

Art Direction

Art Direction

I decided to find semi-realistic food illustration with sparkling effect and vibrant colour to make my Visual Hook (Bak Kut Teh) stand out more. Visually Bak Kut Teh is dull looking as It has monotone colours however I planned to effects such as gradient, grain and effects to make it more visually appealing. I want to set the right mood and timeline for Bak Kut Teh therefore I decided to use graininess effect to bring out some sort of old flim effect. I want the place to be set in the past, in order to do that I decided to use monotonous brown colour palette to create that setting.

MINDS Annual Report Tender

Introduction

Movement for the Intellectually Disabled of Singapore (MINDS) is a voluntary welfare organisation based in Singapore, that provides a wide range of services and programmes for persons with intellectual disabilities (PWIDs) and their families across life stages.


Target Audience

MINDS stakeholders (Board, members, volunteers, caregivers, staff), General Public


Objective

To propose the ideal design that is able to capture the essence of their tagline: Inspiring MINDS, Transforming Lives. To engage readers with contents that will portray a positive image of persons with Intellectual DIsabilities leading empowered lives


My Involvement

I have to conceptualize and design the Annual Report Pages. I have to ensure design and visuals is visually attractive and the contents should convey the intended message with interactive elements to enhance reading/viewing experience.

American Express Advertisement

Introduction

American Express Company is an American multinational corporation specialized in payment card services. However American Express Cards are generally perceived to not have wide acceptance by the merchants in Singapore.


Target Audience

American Express Cardholders & General Public looking for promotions using credit cards.


Objective

To launch Branding campaign / Social Media Advertisements as well as EDM to: Increase the awareness and build stronger brand presence about the surprisingly wide coverage of the American Express Cards in Singapore. To reverse the negative perceptions that the public have. To acquire card sign-ups and increase usage rates of the American Express cards of existing Cardmembers.


My Involvement

To create and conceptualize campaign key visuals, social media advertisement as well as create EDMs to attract and remind American Express's User.

Brand Campaign

Key Visuals for Campaign

How it come about

To promote Travelling using American Express Card, we have to research on what people might be doing during travel.

When we were given the task, we came up with 5 keywords Wander, Curiousity , Wonder , Delight and Unexplored. We search up for images that would match these keywords in order to fit the campaign better.

Since This Campaign have been on-going (the 5th time now) for awhile. We followed the typography from the other version in order to keep things consistent for our users and not to confuse them thinking it's another seperate campaign.

The Key Visual will then be used in every deliverable such as Campaign Landing Page, EDMs, Social Media Advertisement.

The Challenges

Easier than it sounds, American Express have many brand guideline on what to do and not to do for pictures like promoting alcohol, no direct posing to the camera in the photos and many other rules to follow.

Photos found online are very limited especially when we have to filter down to looking for photos that includes people of Asian Ethinicity.

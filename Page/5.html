Benneth Tea Brand Design Packaging

Background Info

We are required to think of a packaging solution for Morning Routine. We decided to do Tea Packaging as we believe we are able to enhance the whole experience of having Tea and we also want to solve problems that users have been facing when drinking tea.

Role

  • To solve the packaging problem of a tea

  • To enhance the whole experience with tea packaging

  • To create a new series dedicated for our conceptualize ideas

Our Research

Research Goals

  • find out the problems user have with Tea Packaging

  • Find ways to improve Tea Packaging

  • Identify Benneth Tea Stylization

Insights

Bennett is a modern brand offering customers high-quality tea for different tastes. They aim to travel the world to bring you curated selections of expertly blended, award-winning teas, carefully crafted to the highest standards. Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot water and dried leaves together. In most countries, tea symbolises peace, enlightenment, refreshment, spiritual and contentment.

Tea Packaging & Benefits

It is very common for one to see tea in a bag as it allow one to enjoy tea in the most convenient way. It has been proven that drinking tea in the morning allow one to be awake and refreshed. Some benefits of drinking tea is cancer prevention and metabolism booster. In a normal tea bag, an estimate of 1.5g-2g of tea is inside which is able to make at least approximately 240ml of tea.


Problem of Tea Packaging

Tea Packaging are often seen as not eco-friendly due to tea bags not being bio-degrable and sometimes staples are used to clip the teabag together which ca potentially be a hazard to users if they choke on the staples.
